ANM Nursing Training Course

(Auxiliary Nurses and Midwifery)

Auxiliary nurse midwife, commonly known as ANM. It is a village-level female health worker in India who is known as the first contact person between the community and the health services. ANMs are regarded as the grass-roots workers in the health organisation pyramid. Their services are considered important to provide safe and effective care to village communities. The role may help communities achieve the targets of national health programmes.

    2. It is a medical nursing training course. During this course, students are given training in nursing related to medicine, in which way they have to take care of patients and how to help them while being treated by the doctor. ANM Nursing Training Course is a diploma course of Nursing field. Girls and women mostly do this course. After doing ANM Nursing Training Course, more jobs are available in the health sector, in which vaccination work is to take care of the patients and all those who come in the course of treatment.
